In main.toml, you may change the inputs read from your controller for forward and backward.
By default, these are A and B respectively, but you may use any of the following digital inputs instead:

0  (A)
1  (B)
2  (X)
3  (Y)
4  (LB/L1)
5  (RB/R1)
6  (back/select)
7  (start)
8  (left stick click)
9  (right stick click)
10 (home)
